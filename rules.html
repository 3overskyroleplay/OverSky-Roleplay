OverSky Roleplay Rules

Please read all rules before joining the server

```

RDM (Random Deathmatch)

Do not kill players without a valid RP reason.

VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch)

Do not use vehicles to intentionally harm players.

Metagaming

Do not use outside game information in roleplay.

Fail RP

Always maintain realistic and serious roleplay behavior.

Fear RP

You must value your character’s life in dangerous situations.
```
